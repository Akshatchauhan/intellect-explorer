# The Problem: Analysis Paralysis

Users were overwhelmed by having 50+ dog options on page one. This led to a **60% drop-off rate** in the first minute.

### The Cognitive Solution

I applied **Hick's Law** (time to make a decision increases with the number and complexity of choices).

1.  **Categorization:** Grouped pets by temperament, not breed.
2.  **Progressive Disclosure:** Showed only 3 options at a time.
